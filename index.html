Welcome to The PalMoji Game!

Inspired by the classic Nintendo Game & Watch series, The PalMoji Game is a test of skill and reflexes. This game is designed to sharpen your attention, train your reaction speed, and develop your logical thinking. To win, you must learn to anticipate the action and make the right decision every time.

How to Play

The goal is simple: catch as many eggs as you can! Control your PalMoji's movement and the position of its basket to catch eggs as they fall from the trays above.
You can play using:

  • On-screen buttons on your mobile device.
  • Keyboard arrow keys.
  • Specific keyboard keys:
    • Q: Top-Left
    • S: Bottom-Left
    • P: Top-Right
    • L: Bottom-Right

Scoring and Penalties

  • Penalty Points: If you miss an egg, it will fall and break. For each broken egg, you receive one penalty point, which appears as a chick on the screen.
  • Special Bonus: If an egg breaks while Bart appears in the window of the little house, you only receive half a penalty point (the chick image will flash).
  • Game Over: The game ends when you accumulate three full penalty points.
  • Clearing Penalties: As you prove your skill, your mistakes are forgiven! Within each block of 1,000 points, your penalties will be cleared when your score reaches the 200 and 500 marks (e.g., at 200, 500, 1200, 1500, 2200, 2500, and so on), announced by a rewarding sound.

Rewards and Recognition

Your skill doesn't go unnoticed! The PalMoji Game rewards dedicated players in several ways:

  • Global Scoreboard: See how you stack up against players from around the world. Climb the ranks and fight for the top spot!
  • Achievement Badges: Unlock special badges for completing challenges and reaching milestones. Earn recognition for high scores, perfect rounds, and consistent daily play.
  • Crypto Rewards: Turn your skills into assets! Top players on the leaderboard and those who conquer special in-game challenges will have the opportunity to earn cryptocurrency rewards, adding a true Play-to-Earn dimension to your gaming experience.

Game Modes & Difficulty

  • Increasing Challenge: The more points you score, the faster and more challenging the game becomes.
  • Game A (Standard): In this mode, eggs will fall from a maximum of three trays at once.
  • Game B (Expert): For a true challenge, this mode features eggs falling from all four trays simultaneously.
  • Max Score: The ultimate goal is to reach the maximum score of 9,999. Can you achieve it?
